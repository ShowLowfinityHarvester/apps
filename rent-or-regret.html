Well... What is renting like?

Renting your first apartment is a can make you feel proud or something similar to that, as you know, it's your first hosue. BUt that also means that have to be responsible with just about everything. You have to sign a lease, pay rent and possibly other fees every month, follow your landlords rules (as you do not own the property), And plan ahead to ensure you can afford rent each month!

  • Rent is not the only cost you are dealing with. you also also pay for utilities among other things they will charge you with.
  • Moving in usually costs more than a month of rent due to there being deposits, and insurance and other costs up front.
  • A lease is a legal agreement. You need to read it thourly thouroughly and understand the the terms and conditions before signing it.
  • Good choices can save money and stress. Bad choices can create problems fast.
